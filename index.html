About Galaxia Adventure Park

Galaxia Adventure Park is a fictional interstellar theme park located in Cape Canaveral, Florida — right beside the stars it's inspired by. Opened in 2075 (in our imagination!), Galaxia spans over 500 acres of cosmic thrills, immersive alien worlds, and zero-gravity adventures. It's our favorite park because no other park combines breathtaking roller coasters with stunning sci-fi storytelling and jaw-dropping special effects. Whether you're 5 or 95, Galaxia has something to leave you starstruck. Every corner of the park is meticulously designed to make you feel like you've truly left Earth behind.

Upcoming Events

  1. Nebula Night Parade (March 15) – A spectacular night parade featuring LED-lit floats inspired by cosmic phenomena.
  2. Alien Encounter Weekend (April 5–6) – Meet and greet with costumed "alien ambassadors," plus exclusive behind-the-scenes tours.
  3. Galactic Food Festival (May 20–22) – Taste dishes from "around the galaxy" — imaginative themed cuisine from 12 different food stations.
  4. Kids' Rocket Launch Day (June 10) – A family event featuring real model rocket launches, science booths, and a junior astronaut certification program.
